ART

Summary

I try to participate in as many art communities as I can. Art means different things to different people and I find it interesting to learn about these different perspectives. Since 2016 I took a break from doing solo shows and focused on the possibilities of street art. Between 2013 and 2021 my life became too hectic and I didn’t keep track of the group shows I was participating in. I still participated in group shows world wide but did not make a record of them.

概要

LIFE

SUMMARY

I have done a lot of work in a lot of different fields. I see work primarily as a way to fund my life and art practice. I also enjoy working a lot of different jobs as I think it makes my art work more interesting. I see it as a way to get perspective of different kinds of life that I would not have gotten otherwise.

LIFE

概要

これまで多くの分野でさまざまな仕事をしてきました。仕事は主に生活やアート活動を支えるための手段と考えています。また、多様な仕事を経験することで、自分の作品がより興味深いものになるとも感じています。それは、通常では得られないさまざまな人生の視点を得る方法でもあります。
