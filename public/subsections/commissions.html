Process

A non-refundable retainer of 33% is required to begin work and hold your commission slot. This covers initial planning and reserves my time. The remaining balance is due on completion, before shipping — payment schedules can be arranged if needed, just ask. If I'm ever unable to complete a commissioned piece, the retainer is fully refunded.

Turnaround time varies by piece and will be discussed individually.
